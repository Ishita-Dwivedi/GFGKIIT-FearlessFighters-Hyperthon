Organizations Making a Difference

World Health Organization (WHO)

The WHO works to promote health, keep the world safe, and serve the vulnerable. They focus on global health challenges like pandemics, immunization, and emergency response.

DonorBox

DonorBox is a fundraising platform helping nonprofits and charities raise money with online donations. They specialize in crowdfunding for impactful causes.

Goonj

Goonj focuses on channeling urban surplus to meet the basic needs of rural communities. Their initiatives aim to empower villages and foster sustainable development.

Oxfam

Oxfam is dedicated to ending poverty and inequality around the world. They work on empowering marginalized communities and addressing climate change.

Journey to Justice

This organization inspires people to take action for social justice through education, storytelling, and art-based initiatives.
